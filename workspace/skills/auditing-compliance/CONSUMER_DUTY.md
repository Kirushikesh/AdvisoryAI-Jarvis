# FCA Consumer Duty: Cross-Cutting Rules for AI Agents

## Rule 1: Act in Good Faith
- **Requirement:** Advice must be honest, fair, and open.
- **Audit Check:** Does the draft hide specific charges or exit fees in the fine print? If yes, FLAG it.

## Rule 2: Avoid Causing Foreseeable Harm
- **Requirement:** You must predict if a client could misunderstand the risk.
- **Audit Check:** - Does the draft imply "Capital Protection" on an investment product? (Harmful).
  - Are "Risk Warnings" (e.g., "Capital at Risk") missing? (Harmful).

## Rule 3: Enable Clients to Pursue Financial Objectives
- **Requirement:** Empower the client, don't just sell.
- **Audit Check:** Does the draft provide a clear "Next Step" or "Call to Action" that helps the client decide?
